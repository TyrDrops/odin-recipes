Cheeseburger Salad

Ingredients:

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Ground meat
  • Burger sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes

Recipe

  1. Preheat a skillet and cook your preffered ground meat option (I like turkey for this but feel free to use a vegan option or beef).
  2. Chop your lettuce while your ground meat cooks. Use a wooden spoon or spatula to break meat apart even more. Season with seasonings, salt, and pepper and cook until meat is cooked through and no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. While your meat is cooking, make your burger sauce. Combine ingredients for sauce in a small bowl or large liquid measuring cup. Mix together until smooth, tasting to see if you want to adjust any of the seasonings.
  4. Top the ground meat and lettuce with the tomatoes, pickles, onions, and cheese. Drizzle your cheeseburger bowls with your desired amount of burger sauce and enjoy.
